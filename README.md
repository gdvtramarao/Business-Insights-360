## Project Overview
Business Insights 360 is an end-to-end **Power BI analytics solution** designed to provide a unified view of business performance across **Finance, Sales, Marketing, and Operations**. Built on **1M+ rows of raw business data**, this project transforms fragmented datasets into a centralized, interactive dashboard that supports informed and data-driven decision-making.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate how large-scale business data can be cleaned, modeled, and visualized to uncover actionable insights and monitor key performance indicators effectively.

---

## Key Features

### Data Modeling & Transformation
- Extracted and transformed raw datasets using **Power Query**.
- Designed an optimized **star-schema data model** for performance and scalability.
- Ensured data accuracy and consistency across multiple business functions.

### Finance Analytics
- Built dynamic **Profit & Loss (P&L) reports**.
- Enabled analysis across **customers, products, countries, and time periods**.
- Provided clear visibility into revenue, costs, and profitability trends.

### Sales & Marketing Analytics
- Analyzed **sales performance and revenue trends** by product and region.
- Evaluated **customer behavior and product performance**.
- Delivered insights to support marketing and sales strategy decisions.

### Operations Analytics
- Monitored key **operational and supply chain KPIs**.
- Identified performance gaps and inefficiencies.
- Supported operational planning through data-driven insights.

### User Experience & Interactivity
- Designed **intuitive dashboards** with easy navigation.
- Implemented **slicers, filters, and drill-through analysis** for deeper exploration.
- Created a user-friendly layout for both technical and non-technical users.
